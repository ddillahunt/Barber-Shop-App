Chapter ThreeBooking an Appointment

Customers can book appointments directly from the homepage. The booking form checks real-time availability to prevent double-booking.

📅 Booking Steps

  1. Scroll to the Book Now section on the homepage (or click "Book Now" in the navigation)
  2. Enter Full Name (required) and Phone Number (required)
  3. Optionally enter an Email address for confirmation
  4. Select a Barber from the dropdown
  5. Choose a Service (Beard Trim, Children's Haircut, Classic Haircut, or Haircut & Shave)
  6. Pick a Preferred Date using the date picker
  7. Select a Preferred Time — unavailable slots are automatically grayed out
  8. Add optional Notes for the barber
  9. Click Book Appointment
⏱ Time Slots: Available every 15 minutes from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM. Slots already booked or blocked by the barber will not appear as available.
✅ Confirmation: After booking, the customer receives an email confirmation (if email was provided) and an SMS confirmation (if phone was provided). The shop also receives a notification.

Chapter FiveAdmin Login

The admin portal is accessible via the /admin/login route. A hidden "Admin" link also exists in the website footer for convenience.

🔒 Logging In

  1. Navigate to the admin login page
  2. Enter your Username and Password
  3. Click Sign In
🛑 Rate Limiting: After 5 failed login attempts, the system locks you out for 30 seconds. A countdown timer is displayed. Wait for it to expire before trying again.
🔐 Security: Credentials are hashed with SHA-256. Sessions are stored in the browser's session storage — closing the browser tab will log you out.

➕ Creating an Appointment (Admin)

  1. Click Add Appointment (or the "+" button)
  2. Fill in: Name, Phone, Barber, Service, Date, Time (Email and Notes are optional)
  3. The system checks for conflicts — it will warn you if the slot is already booked or blocked
  4. Click Save — notifications are sent to the shop and customer

✏ Editing an Appointment

  1. Click the Edit button on any appointment row
  2. Modify the desired fields
  3. Click Save — the customer receives an update notification with the old and new details

🗑 Deleting Appointments

Delete individual appointments or use bulk select to check multiple rows and delete them all at once.

✅ Mark as Completed: Toggle the completed status on any appointment to track which customers have been served.
📅 Calendar View: Switch to the calendar view for a monthly visualization. Click any date to see that day's appointments. Navigate between months with the arrow buttons.

Chapter EightManaging Barbers

The barber management section lets you add, edit, and remove barbers from the system. Barbers appear in the booking dropdown for customers to select.

➕ Adding a Barber

  1. Click Add Barber
  2. Enter the barber's Name, Phone, and Email
  3. Upload a Profile Photo (optional)
  4. Click Save

✏ Editing a Barber

  1. Click the Edit button next to the barber
  2. Update their information or upload a new photo
  3. Click Save
⚠ Deleting a Barber: Removing a barber will not delete their existing appointments, but they will no longer appear as an option for new bookings.

Chapter NineBlocking Time Slots

Block specific time slots or entire date ranges to prevent customers from booking during unavailable periods (breaks, vacations, days off).

📅 Single Day Block

  1. Select the Barber and Date
  2. Use the time range selector (From/To) or click All Day to block every slot
  3. Optionally select individual time slots from the grid — Blue = already booked, Orange = already blocked, Red = selected to block
  4. Add an optional reason
  5. Click Save

📅 Date Range Block

  1. Select the Barber
  2. Choose a Start Date and End Date (max 60-day range)
  3. Add an optional reason (e.g., "Vacation")
  4. Click Save — all time slots for every day in the range are blocked
🔄 Managing Blocks: Existing blocked times can be edited (change dates or times) or deleted to reopen availability.

Chapter ElevenExporting Data

The admin dashboard provides two export options for appointment records.

📄 Export as CSV

Download all appointment data as a comma-separated values file. Opens in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet application. Includes all fields: name, email, phone, barber, service, date, time, notes, status, source, and creation date.

🗎 Export as PDF

Generate a formatted appointment log as a printable PDF. Opens in a new window with Print and Close buttons. Includes the shop header, generation timestamp, and total appointment count.

Chapter FourteenFrequently Asked Questions

💬 Do customers need to book online?
No — walk-ins are always welcome! The online booking system is an added convenience. Customers can also call (508) 872-5556 to check availability.
💬 What payment methods are accepted?
Cash only. Customers should bring cash to their appointment.
💬 Can a customer book without an email?
Yes. Only the name and phone number are required. Email is optional but recommended for receiving confirmations.
💬 How do I block a barber's day off?
Go to the Block Time section, select the barber and date, click "All Day", and save. For vacations, use the Date Range Block feature.
💬 What happens if a customer books a conflicting time?
The system automatically prevents double-booking. Unavailable time slots are grayed out and cannot be selected.
💬 How do I access the admin dashboard?
Navigate to /admin/login or click the "Admin" link in the website footer. Enter your username and password to sign in.
💬 Can I export my appointment data?
Yes. The admin dashboard offers both CSV and PDF export options for all appointment records.
💬 Is the website available in Spanish?
Yes! The entire customer-facing site is available in Spanish at the /es routes. Customers can switch languages from the navigation.
💬 I got locked out after too many login attempts.
Wait 30 seconds for the lockout timer to expire, then try again with the correct credentials.
