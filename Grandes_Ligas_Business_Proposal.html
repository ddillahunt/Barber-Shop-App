1. Executive Summary

Grandes Ligas is a premium barber shop located in Framingham, Massachusetts, delivering expert grooming services for the modern gentleman. With a team of four highly experienced barbers and a combined 45+ years of expertise, Grandes Ligas offers classic cuts, contemporary styles, and full grooming transformations in a welcoming, bilingual (English/Spanish) environment.

Our mission is to provide an exceptional grooming experience that blends traditional barbering craftsmanship with modern styling techniques, creating a destination where every client leaves looking sharp and feeling confident.

4. Services & Pricing

Classic Haircut - $30

Duration: 30-45 minutes

Traditional cuts with precision and style. Includes:

  • Personal consultation with your barber
  • Professional shampoo and conditioning
  • Precision scissor and/or clipper cutting
  • Detailed edge work and line-up
  • Premium styling products
  • Complimentary beverage during service

Our Classic Haircut is the foundation of traditional barbering excellence. Whether you're looking for a timeless business cut, a clean fade, or a stylish taper, our expert barbers will craft a look that suits your personality and lifestyle.

Premium Cut & Style - $45

Duration: 45-60 minutes

Complete styling with premium products. Includes:

  • In-depth style consultation
  • Luxury shampoo and deep conditioning treatment
  • Expert precision cutting
  • Professional blow-dry and styling
  • High-end styling products application
  • Scalp massage
  • Hot towel treatment

This comprehensive package combines expert cutting with advanced styling techniques to create a sophisticated, polished look. Every step is designed to exceed expectations.

Beard Trim & Shape - $25

Duration: 20-30 minutes

Expert beard grooming and maintenance. Includes:

  • Beard consultation and design
  • Precise trimming and shaping
  • Edge definition and cleanup
  • Beard oil or balm application
  • Styling and grooming tips
  • Hot towel finish

Our skilled barbers understand the art of beard sculpting, working with natural growth patterns and facial structure to create a shape that enhances your features.

Full Grooming Package - $65

Duration: 60-75 minutes

The ultimate men's grooming experience. Includes:

  • Complete style consultation
  • Premium haircut with styling
  • Professional beard trim and shaping
  • Luxury hot towel treatment
  • Scalp and facial massage
  • Premium product application
  • Complimentary beverage
  • Grooming recommendations

This is more than just a haircut and beard trim - it's a full grooming transformation designed for special occasions or when you want to treat yourself to the best.

6. Market Opportunity

Target Market

  • Men aged 18-55 in the Framingham, MA metropolitan area
  • Professionals seeking premium grooming services
  • Spanish-speaking community members (bilingual service is a key differentiator)
  • Walk-in and appointment-based clients

Competitive Advantages

  1. Bilingual Service: Full English and Spanish support creates an inclusive, welcoming environment for the diverse Framingham community
  2. Experienced Team: 45+ combined years of barbering experience across four specialists
  3. Extended Hours: Open until 8:00 PM on weekdays, accommodating working professionals
  4. Walk-In Friendly: No appointment required, reducing barriers to service
  5. Specialized Expertise: Each barber has a distinct specialty, ensuring clients receive expert service for their specific needs
  6. Premium Experience: Complimentary beverages, hot towel treatments, and scalp massages elevate the grooming experience beyond a standard haircut
  7. Modern Digital Presence: Professional website with online booking, service details, and team profiles

Location Advantage

Framingham, MA is a diverse, growing community with a significant Hispanic/Latino population. Grandes Ligas is strategically positioned to serve this community with culturally relevant, bilingual services while also appealing to the broader market seeking premium grooming.
